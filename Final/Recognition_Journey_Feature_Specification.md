# Recognition Journey Feature Specification

## Overview
Recognition Journey is an annual, story-driven experience that transforms employee recognition data into a beautiful, shareable digital book.

## Objectives
- Celebrate employee achievements
- Increase engagement
- Encourage recognition culture
- Enable social sharing
- Premium monetizable experience

## Entry Point
Employee logs into the web application.
A lightweight feed notification displays:
**Your 2025 Recognition Journey is Ready!**
CTA: **Open Journey**

Selecting the CTA opens a full-screen story viewer.

## User Journey
1. Cover
2. Your Impact
3. Recognition Milestones
4. Recognitions From People
5. Values You Represent
6. Badge Cabinet
7. Award Cabinet
8. How You Built Culture
9. Closing Page

## Navigation
- One page visible at a time
- Previous & Next navigation
- Progress indicator at top
- ESC closes viewer
- Responsive desktop-first

## Page Details

### Cover
Employee photo, name, joining year, edition year, book cover.

### Your Impact
Cards:
- Recognitions
- People Recognised Me
- Words of Recognition
- Company Values Represented

### Recognition Milestones
Joined company milestone plus yearly milestones.

### Recognitions From People
Manager, Peers, Cross Team, Direct Reports.

### Values You Represent
Only values actually received.
Top 6 values by recognition count.
No progress bars.

### Badge Cabinet
Grid sorted by highest badge count.
Footer: Total Badges Earned.

### Award Cabinet
Grid sorted by highest award count.
Footer: Total Awards Received.

### How You Built Culture
Cards:
- Recognitions Given
- People Recognised
- Comments Made
- Likes Given

Footer:
Growth grows when it's shared.

### Closing
Headline:
Your Journey Inspires Many

Subtext:
Keep growing. Keep inspiring.
The best is yet to come!

## Empty States
- Joining milestone always shown
- Hide unavailable awards/badges/values
- Adapt layout automatically

## UI Specifications
- Story aspect ratio: 9:16
- Font: Inter
- Background: #F8F7FF
- Primary Purple: #5B3DF5
- Accent Gold: #F5C84C
- Rounded corners: 20px
- Soft shadows
- Minimal white space
- Center aligned story

## Download
Allow downloading each page individually as PNG for social sharing.
